In LuckDAO everyone is a winner!

LuckDAO will send each round of the lottery to Mint users in the form of an NFT mystery box. Each NFT mystery box is a lottery ticket. When the NFT mystery box is opened, a winner will be born. LuckDAO will give the funds obtained from the NFT Mint to the winners in either ETH, Lucky Coin, or other equivalent to the prizes. If the funds obtained by LuckDAO in each round of Mint do not reach the value of the corresponding prizes, 50% of the total funds will be returned to the winners. Everyone will receive an airdrop of Lucky Coin, which can be used for the next round of Mystery Boxes.
